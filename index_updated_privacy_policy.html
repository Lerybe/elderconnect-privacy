Privacy Policy — ElderConnect

1. Introduction

ElderConnect is a family video calling application developed by LeRybe Tech to help family members communicate more easily with elderly relatives. This Privacy Policy explains what information ElderConnect collects, how that information is used, stored, protected, and deleted, and what choices and rights users have regarding their personal data.

2. Data We Collect

Data Purpose
Username / display name Display identity within the app and group
Email address Account authentication, invitation, password reset, and account-related communication
Family group membership Connecting users within the correct family group
User role (Admin, Family Caller, Elder) Providing role-based access and features
Online / offline status Showing device or user availability to relevant family members
Device notification token(s) (such as FCM token) Sending call and app notifications to the correct device
Temporary call / session records Managing active or recent call setup and session routing

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To enable video calls between family members and elderly relatives
  • To manage family group membership and role-based access
  • To support caller, admin, and elder device flows within the app
  • To send call notifications and service-related notifications to devices
  • To support password reset and account authentication
  • To process account deletion requests and remove relevant associated data
  • To maintain service integrity, security, and abuse prevention where required

3.1 Elder Device Setup and Consent

In some cases, an ElderConnect device may be configured by a family member or administrator (“Admin”) on behalf of an elder user.

By completing the Elder Device Setup process, the Admin confirms that:

  • the elder user is aware of the use of ElderConnect for communication with family members, and
  • the elder user agrees to receive such communications through the service.

During setup, a confirmation record may be stored as part of the elder user’s account data.

This confirmation is used solely to ensure transparency and compliance with applicable privacy requirements.

4. Data Storage

ElderConnect stores app data using Google Firebase services, including Firebase Authentication and Firebase Realtime Database. Data is hosted in Belgium (europe-west1). Access to data is restricted through authentication and application security rules.

5. Data Retention

  • User account and group-related data is retained while the account or device remains active in ElderConnect.
  • Temporary call / session records are retained only as needed for call handling and related service operation.
  • When an account or managed family group is deleted, associated app data is removed in accordance with the deletion flow applicable to that role.
  • User data, including elder setup confirmation records, is retained only while the account is active and is deleted when the account or associated group is removed, except where limited retention is required for legal or operational purposes.

6. Account Deletion and Data Deletion

ElderConnect provides users with ways to request deletion of their account and associated app data.

How to request deletion

  • In the app: users can access account deletion from the relevant account/settings screen in the app.
  • On the web: users may also use the public account deletion page: ElderConnect Account Deletion
  • By email: users may send a deletion request to michael.rybski@gmail.com.

Role-based deletion behavior

  • Family Caller deletion: removes the caller account and associated app data relevant to that account.
  • Elder deletion: removes the elder account/device association and associated app data relevant to that elder profile or device.
  • Admin deletion: may permanently delete the Admin account and the managed family group, including associated Caller and Elder accounts within that group.

Data that may be deleted

Depending on the role and deletion scenario, deleted data may include:

  • account credentials or authentication association
  • username / profile information
  • family group membership references
  • role associations
  • device notification token(s)
  • temporary call / session data related to the deleted account or group, where applicable

Data that may be retained

Limited information may be retained only where necessary for security, fraud prevention, abuse prevention, legal compliance, regulatory obligations, dispute handling, or lawful enforcement requests. Any such retained information will be limited to what is reasonably necessary for those purposes.

Consent Data Handling

When an elder account or family group is deleted, all associated data — including any consent confirmation records — is deleted together with the elder account data.

Important: If an Admin deletes a managed family group, associated Caller and Elder accounts in that group may also be permanently deleted.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, including if you are in the European Union or Israel, you may have rights regarding your personal data, such as the right to:

  • access your personal data
  • request correction of inaccurate data
  • request deletion of your data
  • object to certain processing, where applicable

To exercise a privacy or deletion-related request, contact: michael.rybski@gmail.com

8. Children

ElderConnect is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal data through the app, please contact us so that we can investigate and take appropriate action.
