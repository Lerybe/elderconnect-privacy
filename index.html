1. Introduction

ElderConnect is a video calling app developed by LeRybe Tech that enables family members to automatically connect with elderly relatives. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use the App.

2. Data We Collect

DataPurpose
UsernameDisplay name within the app
Email addressAccount authentication and password reset
Family group membershipConnecting family members within the app
Online/offline statusShowing availability to family members
Device notification token (FCM)Enabling incoming call notifications
Call recordsTemporary call session management

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To enable video calls between family members and elderly relatives
  • To manage family group membership
  • To send call notifications to devices
  • To allow password reset via email

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Realtime Database and Authentication), hosted in Belgium (europe-west1). Firebase complies with GDPR requirements. Data is protected using Firebase Security Rules that restrict access to authenticated users only.

5. Data Retention

  • User data is retained until the account is deleted by a group admin
  • Call records are retained temporarily during active sessions
  • When an account is deleted, all associated data is automatically removed

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union or Israel, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to data processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at: michael.rybski@gmail.com

7. Children

This App is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

8. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase — authentication, database, and notifications (Google Privacy Policy)
  • Metered.ca — WebRTC TURN server for video call routing

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes via the App. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
